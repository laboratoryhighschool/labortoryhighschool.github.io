EVENTS IN THE LABORATORY
HIGH SCHOOL

Show your excitement in these coming activities!

Citizens Army Training Tactical Inspection

In this event, the Grade 10 Students will exhibit their discipline and unison just like what they have learned as trainees during the past months. Several inspectors from the Armed Forces of the Philippines will visit on this momentous half-day event.

Grade 12 Recollection

On May 28, the Graduating Batch of the Laboratory High School will be having their In-House Recollection. During this event, the Grade 12 Students will reminisce the memories that they shared with their batchmates all throughout their six-year stay in the Laboratory High School.

3rd Senior High School Commencement Exercises, Grade 10 Moving Up Ceremony, Laboratory High School Recognition Day

In these events, the hardwork and perseverance of the students for this academic year will be paid off and rewarded. These special events are special not only to the students themselves, but also to their parents, and their teachers.
